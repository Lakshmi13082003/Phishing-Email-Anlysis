Phishing Email Analysis Report
******************************

Sample Phishing Email
*********************

From: support@paypa1.com
Subject: Urgent! Your Account Will Be Suspended

Dear Customer,

We detected unusual activity in your account.
Please click the link below to verify your account immediately:

http://paypal-verification-alert.com/login

If you do not verify within 24 hours, your account will be permanently suspended.

Thank you,
PayPal Support Team

******************************************************************************

## 1. Sender Address Analysis
The sender email is support@paypa1.com.
The domain is fake because it uses number "1" instead of "l".
This indicates email spoofing.

## 2. Urgency and Threat Language
The email uses threatening language such as:
- "Urgent"
- "Account will be suspended"
This is a common phishing tactic.

## 3. Suspicious Link
The link provided is:
http://paypal-verification-alert.com/login

This is not the official PayPal website.
It is a fake domain.

## 4. Generic Greeting
The email starts with "Dear Customer" instead of the user's real name.

## 5. Conclusion
This email contains multiple phishing indicators including spoofed domain, urgent language, suspicious URL, and generic greeting.
Therefore, it is a phishing email.
